The Witch’s Curse

Long ago, the Witch of Little Turtle Hollow wove a spell so deep it seeped into the walls, binding this house to her will. But there is hope. Hidden within these rooms lies ancient knowledge—fragments of lost lore left behind by those who once stood against her.

These remnants hold the counterspells said to weaken her magic. Seek them out. Decipher their meaning. Each counterspell will provide guidance on where the next awaits.

Only when all is reclaimed may the path become clear. Bind the witch's curse and let the final ritual commence.

Before all else, seek the Raven for vital information.
